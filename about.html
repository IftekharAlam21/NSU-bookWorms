NSU-BOOKWorms is a (dynamic, responsive , and Interactive ) platform designed for the students of North South University to lend, borrow and sale their books. Our primary concern is non-academic books ( story books, novel, poems etc).But you can also sale the academic books that you are done with. To be a member of Bookworms, you first have to register and then you can decide whether you want to give your books for sale or rent. Definitely you also can lend from someone; But all after registering with us which is completely free !
